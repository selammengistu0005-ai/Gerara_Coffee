Our Story

Located in the heart of Adama, Gerara Coffee offers an escape into a world of premium Ethiopian coffee and elegant relaxation. We believe in creating a friendly atmosphere where exquisite taste meets beautiful presentation.

Whether you're looking for the ideal place to meet friends, work in a relaxing environment, or simply unwind with our fresh desserts and refreshing iced beverages, Gerara Coffee welcomes you.
